Frequently Asked Questions

How does remote support work?

It's simple and secure! I use a trusted remote desktop application (like TeamViewer or AnyDesk). You'll download a small program that gives me one-time, secure access to your computer. You can see everything I'm doing on your screen, and you can end the session at any time. Once we're done, I can't access your computer again without your permission.

Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely. Your privacy is my top priority. I only access the parts of your system necessary to fix the issue. The remote connection is encrypted, and I will never access, copy, or look at your personal files unless you specifically ask me to for data recovery purposes.
